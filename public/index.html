STORY   😄💙🙌😎

We love our iPhones as much as you, so a few friends and I started Tiny Hearts studio. We got to make careers from our passion for apps and our parents stopped telling us to get jobs (so that was nice).

Fast forward a few years and we’ve made some pretty cool apps, won some awards, and even had an app featured in an Apple commercial. This momentum encouraged us to dive into our next big endeavour. When Apple opened their doors to custom keyboards with iOS 8, there was no doubt: we had to make the Next Keyboard.

We know what we want in the perfect iOS keyboard and the problems we want to solve. But we also wanted to make you part of the process. So we reached out to the community at Reddit, Product Hunt, and Designer News. We even held workshops at meetups and interviewed users to make sure we were building something people want. What came next was 7 months of coding, designing, building, and testing, all the while getting more feedback and excitement for Next’s launch.
